Part I: Definition of Streaming

Streaming can be defined as the playback of a multimedia file that can be played without the need to download it, with the use of the internet. There are 2 types of streaming media:

  • On-Demand Streaming

    • It allows the audience or viewers to view their selected content whenever they want, with the ability to pause, fast-forward, rewind, or otherwise manipulate the media they're consuming.

  • Live Streaming

    • On the other hand, live streaming the opposite of the above, because whereas content for On-Demand Streaming is pre-recorded, content for Live Streaming is broadcasted in real time, hence the word "Live".

    • Usually, content for live Live Streaming cannot be played backwards, but some platforms for consuming said content allows for it.

b. Music Video

A music video is a videotaped performance of a recorded popular song, usually accompanied by dancing and visual images interpreting the lyrics. Although the origins of music videos began with “musical short films” that first appeared in the 1920s, they really came into prominence in the 1980s when MTV created their channel around the medium and it changed how we received our music.[14]

  • Example of a music video: Link

According to a survey conducted in Italy in 2020, more women than men listened to music every day in their free time. As reported by the data, 38 percent of female respondents claimed to listen to music every day. By contrast, 35 percent of men did the same as of the survey period. Moreover, 13 percent of female interviewees said to never listen to music in their free time. Conversely, 10 percent of male respondents claimed to not listen to music at all as of 2020.[15]

In 2015, online music videos in the United Kingdom started getting age ratings from the British Board of Film Classification (BBFC) in the same way films do, following a UK Government pilot. Of the 132 videos submitted to the BBFC so far, 56 are rated 12 and 53 classified 15. Only one video got an 18 certificate - Dizzee Rascal's Couple Of Stack.[16]
